How to use this site

Every section of the workshop becomes an interactive dashboard. The site works on screen in the room and stands alone for revision afterwards — and everything you type is saved for you.

Two Modes How It Flows Your Answers FAQ
Two modes, one site

Present or self-paced

Use the toggle in the top-right of every page. It changes how much explanation shows, without changing the content.

Self-paced

For revisiting before or after the workshop. Fuller explanations, “why this matters” notes, and private answer capture. Dashboards teach the concept on their own.

Present

For driving on screen in the room. Larger visuals, less body text, big interactive moments. Ideal when your facilitator is leading the discussion.

How it flows

A dashboard for every section

The course runs in order, but you can jump anywhere from the hub. Each module page mirrors the workbook — same sections, same activities — turned into something you interact with.

1

Start at the hub

The home page maps both days. Each card names the dashboard that teaches that section.

2

Work each dashboard

Drag, classify, rate, build. The interaction is the learning — the “sections covered” rail shows how it maps to the workbook.

3

Carry your workflow

The workflow you pick in Module 1 follows you through every later module and into your plan.

Your answers

Everything saves automatically

Anything you type is saved to your device as you go — no account, no sign-in. It’s private to this browser. At the end, your canvas, stance card, guardrails, incident response and first moves are all gathered into My Leadership Plan, which you can print or save as a PDF.

Because it’s stored on your device, use the same browser to keep your work — and you can clear it any time from the plan page.

Open My Leadership Plan →
Tip for facilitators. In Present mode you can fill a shared example live on screen. For the room’s own work, learners use their own devices in Self-paced mode so each person keeps their plan.
FAQ

Quick questions

Do I need an account?
No. Nothing is sent anywhere — your answers live in this browser on your device.
Will my answers sync across devices?
Not in this version. Use the same browser to keep your work, and print your plan to PDF if you want a permanent copy.
Do I have to do it in order?
No — jump anywhere from the hub. But the course is designed as an arc, and a few activities build on earlier ones (your Module 1 workflow, for example).
What if a 3D visual doesn’t load?
Every 3D scene has a clean 2D fallback, so the dashboard still works without it.
